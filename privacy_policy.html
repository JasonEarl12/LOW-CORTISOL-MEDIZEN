Dashboard Privacy Policy

This policy explains how data is handled within the Medizen system dashboards for both users and administrators, ensuring compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1. User Dashboard Privacy

Users accessing the system dashboard are provided with secure and private access to their own personal and medical information. This includes appointment details, prescriptions, and health records. The system ensures that users can only view and manage their own data, preventing unauthorized access to other individuals' records.

Users are responsible for safeguarding their login credentials, including usernames and passwords. Any activity performed under a user account is recorded by the system for security monitoring and auditing purposes.

2. Administrator Dashboard Privacy

Administrators are granted controlled access to system data based on their assigned roles. Through Role-Based Access Control (RBAC), administrators can only access the information necessary to perform their duties, such as managing patient records, scheduling, and system maintenance.

All administrative actions are logged and monitored to ensure accountability and prevent misuse of data. Administrators must comply with strict confidentiality policies and legal standards, and any violation may result in disciplinary or legal consequences.

3. Data Security Measures

The system implements strong security measures including encrypted connections, secure data storage, and session management to protect all information within the dashboards. Continuous monitoring is conducted to detect and prevent unauthorized access or potential security threats.
