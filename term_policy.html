Data Privacy & Protection Notice — RA 10173

This notice explains in detail how Medizen Health & Wellness Centre collects, processes, stores, and protects your personal information in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173). It outlines your rights as a data subject and the responsibilities of the organization in safeguarding your information.

1. Purpose and Scope

This Privacy Notice defines the policies and procedures followed by Medizen in handling personal and sensitive data. It ensures that all collected information is used only for legitimate healthcare and operational purposes while maintaining strict confidentiality and compliance with applicable Philippine laws and regulations.

2. Definitions

Data Subject: Refers to any individual whose personal, medical, or identifying information is collected and processed by the clinic system.

Processing: Includes any action performed on personal data such as collection, recording, organization, storage, updating, retrieval, or deletion.

RBAC: A security approach that restricts system access based on the roles and responsibilities assigned to authorized personnel.

3. Data Collection

Identity
Includes full legal name, gender, date of birth, and other identifying information required for accurate patient identification.
Contact
Includes residential address, phone numbers, and email addresses for communication and appointment coordination.
Clinical
Includes medical history, diagnoses, prescriptions, and laboratory results necessary for treatment and care.
System Logs
Includes login timestamps and user activities for monitoring, auditing, and maintaining system security.

The collection of sensitive personal information is strictly regulated under RA 10173 Section 13 to ensure lawful and secure handling.

4. Legal Basis

Personal data is processed based on valid legal grounds including medical necessity for patient care, administrative requirements for clinic operations, regulatory compliance with the Department of Health, and explicit consent provided by the patient when required.

5. Security

Medizen implements multiple layers of security including encrypted data transmission, secure storage systems, and controlled access through Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) to prevent unauthorized access, data breaches, or misuse of sensitive information.

6. Retention

All records are retained for a period of fifteen (15) years following the last patient interaction, in accordance with DOH Administrative Order No. 2005-0014. After this period, data is securely disposed of to prevent unauthorized recovery or access.

7. Rights

As a data subject, you have the right to access your personal information, request corrections for inaccuracies, demand deletion where applicable, and obtain a copy of your data for transfer purposes under RA 10173.
